Shrek Character Info
Shrek

Shrek is a green ogre with a fearsome appearance that comes along
with his large stature, but despite this giant's intimidating presence,
he holds a kind heart withn him willing to help his friends and family
through whatever problem they have.
Donkey

Like his buddy Shrek, Donkey is a good natures being willing to
help friends and family, enlightening the mood if needed with
his energetic and talkative attitude, he keeps the group lively
and up on their spirits despite being stated as "annoying" from time to time.
Princess Fiona

Princess Fiona, as a character, defies the princess stereotype and male patriarchy.
Being the female lead, Princess Fiona constantly she presents a dominating
presence challenging stereotypes with her taking matters into her own hands
and taking initiative in crucial points of the story with her confidence and enhanced physical abilities as an ogre.
